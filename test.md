> Because without a framework, we have three problems. One: **security**. Sensitive data leaking into uncontrolled prompts. Two: **compliance**. GDPR, industry regulations, governance — we're AXA, not a startup. And three: **scaling**. How do we make this work for everyone, not just a few early adopters?

## SLIDE 3 — Our Solution *(45 sec)*

> Our approach is built on two pillars: **Skills** and **Inner Sourcing**.
>
> A Skill is a module that gives AI precise business instructions. Inner Sourcing is the sharing mechanism — like open source, but inside the company. And the outcome? Real productivity gains, with full governance.
>
> In practice: we create, we share, we reuse. All of it secure.
>
> Let me break it down.

**[Click]**

---

## SLIDE 4 — What is a Skill? *(55 sec)*

> A Skill is simple. It's a folder — containing best practices, specific instructions, and templates — that tells AI *how* to produce a professional-grade deliverable.
>
> Take an example: the "PPTX" Skill. It contains everything the AI needs to generate a clean PowerPoint — structure, layout, font sizes, content logic. We can build Skills for anything: Word documents, PDFs, reports, branding, you name it.
>
> The payoff? Consistent quality. Brand compliance. Reusable by every team. And most importantly — versioned and auditable. We know exactly what the AI is working with.

**[Click]**

---

## SLIDE 5 — What is Inner Sourcing? *(50 sec)*

> Now, a Skill sitting alone in a folder has no value. The value comes when you share it.
>
> That's Inner Sourcing: we apply open-source principles inside AXA. One team creates a Skill, publishes it to an internal catalogue. Another team picks it up, improves it, contributes back. It's a virtuous cycle.
>
> The benefits are tangible: shared effort across entities, faster time-to-value, and most importantly — centralized governance. No more shadow AI. Everything is tracked, everything is compliant.

**[Click]**

---

## SLIDE 6 — Security Validation Process *(55 sec)*

> And this is where I want to reassure everyone in the room.
>
> No Skill gets published without validation. The process is clear, four steps. A team creates a Skill. Then a **dedicated security team** audits it — we check data handling, compliance with AXA policies, GDPR alignment, code safety. After review, it gets approved. And only then is it published and available to everyone.
>
> **No Skill goes live without approval.** That's our golden rule. That's what makes this system trustworthy — and that's why business teams can use it with full confidence.

**[Click]**

Step two: the system detects it's AXA and automatically loads the Branding Skill. Step three: the AI generates the deck — correct colors, logo in the right place, Arial Black typography, compliant layouts. Step four: the .pptx file is ready, on-brand, good to go.

## Delivery Notes

- **Tempo**: conversational, not scripted. Talk like you're explaining to a colleague over coffee.
- **Slide 2**: lean into "we're AXA, not a startup" — it anchors the security need and gets a smile.
- **Slide 6**: slow down on "No Skill goes live without approval" — this is THE line for decision-makers. Make eye contact.
- **Slide 9**: this is your showstopper. Real pause before the reveal. 2-3 seconds of silence after "entirely built by a Skill." Let the audience connect the dots themselves.
- **Never read the slides** — everything in this script is complementary, not a repeat of what's on screen.
- **Total runtime**: ~7 min with natural transitions and breathing room.
